1996/05/09

Hello everyone, it's been a while since my last update. I apologize for my absence, but I've been in a bit of a tough spot lately. As you might remember, I expressed some concerns in my last post about being followed and watched by unknown individuals.

Well, unfortunately, things didn't get any better. In fact, they got worse. A lot worse. I started to feel like I was living in a paranoid dream. Everywhere I went, I was convinced that someone was watching me. I couldn't sleep at night. I couldn't focus on anything else but my fear.

Eventually, I had to make a difficult decision. I had to leave my old life behind and start fresh somewhere else. It wasn't an easy decision, but it was the only way I could feel safe again.

Now, I'm living in a new place, with new people, and a new identity. I don't want to reveal too much, for fear that they might be reading this right now. But suffice it to say, I'm far away from my old life and trying to start over.

But the fear never truly goes away. I'm still paranoid, still looking over my shoulder, still wondering if I'm being watched. I don't know if I'll ever feel safe again. All I know is that I can't stop fighting. I can't let them win.

Unfortunately, I have come to the realization that I am unable to continue this blog. I will have to leave it behind, as it is too risky to continue. But I hope that my message has reached some of you, and that you will carry on the fight for truth and justice. Remember, even if they can silence one voice, they cannot silence us all.

Thank you for your support, and please, stay safe.
