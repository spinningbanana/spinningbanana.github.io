1996/05/09

I need to talk to you all about something important. Something scary. Something that has been weighing on my mind for weeks now.

You see, after I wrote my last post about the dangers of bananas and the benefits of apples, something strange started happening. I began to receive anonymous phone calls and emails, warning me to stop spreading my "lies" and "propaganda." At first, I brushed it off as just a few crazies who didn't like my anti-banana message. But then things started to get really weird.

I noticed strange cars parked outside my house at odd hours of the day and night. I caught glimpses of men in suits following me around town. My computer was hacked, and all of my files were deleted. And then, just last night, I received a warning from an anonymous source that the government was after me.

I know it sounds crazy. I know it sounds like something out of a spy movie. But the more I think about it, the more I realize that I might be in real danger. The government has a lot to lose if people start questioning the safety of bananas. After all, the banana industry is worth billions of dollars, and any negative press could hurt their profits.

But I can't just sit back and let this happen. I can't let them silence me or intimidate me into submission. I believe in the power of information and education, and I will continue to spread the truth about bananas and apples, no matter what.

So, if something happens to me, if I suddenly disappear or stop posting, please know that I didn't go willingly. Please know that I will never stop fighting for what I believe in. And please, for your own safety, be careful about what you say and who you trust. The world is a dangerous place, and sometimes the truth is the most dangerous thing of all.
